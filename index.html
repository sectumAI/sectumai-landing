Smart Demand History Cleaning

Your historical sales data is the foundation of accurate forecasting, but raw e-commerce data is often corrupted by stockouts, promotions, and data collection issues.

What We Fix:

  • Out-of-Stock Periods: Detect when zero sales actually mean lost demand due to stockouts
  • Outlier Detection: Identify and handle unusual spikes from promotions or data errors
  • Missing Data: Fill gaps in your sales history using intelligent interpolation
  • Seasonal Adjustments: Normalize data for holidays and seasonal effects
  • Promotional Impact: Separate base demand from promotional lift

Result: Clean, reliable demand history that improves forecast accuracy by 20-30% compared to using raw data.
